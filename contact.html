CONTACTS

Feel free to contact me anytime — whether you just want to say hello, ask me for travel advice or if you’re interested in collaborating on something fun! I make it a goal to respond to every email, but please understand that it may take a few days if I’m traveling in a remote place or I don’t have access to wifi.

Due to the high volume of emails I get daily, I’d appreciate if you could ask specific questions and keep your messages under 1 paragraph (2-3 sentences). As much as I’d love to hear your life story, I simply don’t have the time. Remember to look at my About Me page and FAQ page, as I’ve probably answered most of your questions already.

If you are interested in working together, then please see my Work With Me Page.
